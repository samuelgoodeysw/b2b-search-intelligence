# B2B Search Intelligence Demo Script

## One-line pitch

B2B Search Intelligence shows what logged-in customers searched for, whether Magento completed the search, and whether they later added or ordered anything matching.

## What to show first

Open:

Reports -> Business Intelligence -> B2B Search Intelligence

Start with the summary cards:

- Total logged-in searches
- Unresolved searches
- Completed searches
- Completion not recorded
- Matched cart items
- Matched orders

## Demo story

Say:

This is not generic keyword analytics.

This is customer-level search intent from logged-in B2B users.

We can see the customer, what they searched, whether Magento completed the search response, and whether they later added or ordered anything matching.

## Row walkthrough

For a successful follow-through row, explain:

This customer searched for leaf spring.

Magento completed the search response.

The customer later added a matching product to cart.

That tells us the search journey continued.

For an unresolved row, explain:

This customer searched for a product or part phrase.

Magento either returned zero results or did not record a completed response.

No later matching cart or order was found.

That makes it a useful search-intent signal for review.

## Why it matters for B2B

B2B customers often search with high-intent language.

They may search by SKU, part number, replacement part, brand, or technical wording.

If those searches do not lead to cart or order activity, the merchant gets a practical review list.

## Strongest value proposition

This helps the merchant answer:

Are logged-in customers telling us what they want, but not successfully reaching or buying it?

## What not to overclaim

Do not say:

- guaranteed lost revenue
- exact lost sales
- proof the customer abandoned only because of search
- replacement for Adobe Live Search or Algolia

Say instead:

- possible unresolved buying intent
- customer-level search signal
- useful review list
- practical B2B search intelligence

## Suggested next step after demo

Offer a focused review:

- install or run against a safe environment
- capture logged-in search events
- review unresolved searches
- identify patterns
- decide whether fixes belong in catalogue data, search config, synonyms, or search platform work
